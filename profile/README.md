## About

Aster Studio is an independent software studio focused on designing high-quality consumer applications that are elegant, intuitive, and purposeful.

We believe software should be:
- Beautiful by default
- Thoughtfully designed
- Technically excellent
- Delightful to use
- Built for the long term

Our products span health, education, creativity, and everyday life, with artificial intelligence used where it genuinely improves the experience.

## Philosophy

We build products that people enjoy opening every day.

Instead of chasing trends, we focus on timeless principles:

- Simplicity over complexity
- Quality over quantity
- Privacy over surveillance
- Craft over speed
- Long-term value over short-term growth
